Our Service Offerings

GBPL strength lies in the core team experience in the various aspects of Nickel Cadmium (NiCad) batteries that covers battery selection, design of battery, installation and post commissioning & servicing of batteries. GBPL strength lies in their innovation of Nickel Cadmium (NiCad) batteries catering to Commercial & Solar/ Home UPS battery market & Heavy Duty Vehicle / DG SET starting application by bringing in 12V battery for the first time in India. GBPL can help to optimize the battery sizing resulting in smaller battery for a given customer specific load, resulting in cost reduction. We ensure our service network is all India basis since most of the batteries are used in core industry like Power plant, refineries, petrochemical plants which are located at remote corners of India.

Quality policy of GBPL

GBPL shall ensure total customer satisfaction by providing quality production and fully meeting the application needs of the customer. GBPL batteries are manufactured with international quality active materials, chemicals & components. Consistency in the product quality will be the main motto of GBPL. GBPL will endeavor constantly in improving and developing/upgrading their products to customer expectation from time to time and for new application.

Company Bio

Gaythri Batteries Private Limited started manufacturing Nickel Cadmium Pocket Plate Cell battery at Bangalore in 2012 with the goal of having only zero defect & reliable product in portfolio.
